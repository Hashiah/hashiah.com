About Us

hashiah /haʃʌɪə/ : a blended word created from meshiah and hash

Beginning

There is not too much to say. The company started in 2018 with the crypto-hype and aimed to produce various product related to that industry. Needless to say, we never got there...

Instead, we operated as consultants, cloud engineers, programmers. This worked for a while, but eventually led to burnout. After a little pause, we have rethought what we want and how we want... dusted off some old ideas and started to finally make them.

Nowadays

In 2024 we terminated all ongoing contracts and started to focus only on own products. The results are yet to see.
